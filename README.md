# RealoKids

RealoKids is a calm companion app for children and parents.

The app helps children build small daily habits through short missions, gentle
encouragement, breathing practice, routines, and parent-approved rewards. A
soft Buddy character stays with the child throughout the experience, offering
support without pressure.

Parents can shape the app around family life by choosing missions, rewards,
morning routine steps, daily schedule items, calming preferences, and things the
child loves. The Parent Zone can be protected with a PIN.

RealoKids is designed to feel warm and playful, but not overstimulating. It
uses small wins, simple choices, optional voice guidance, and quiet celebration
moments to help children feel capable.

Current highlights:

- Daily missions for movement, care, tidying, calm, and connection
- Parent-managed rewards and optional “before reward” mission bridges
- Morning routine and day schedule support
- Breathing together with Buddy
- Optional text-to-speech guidance
- Age-aware mission wording and tiny facts
- Parent-authored child preferences for motivation and comfort
- Custom pictures for missions, rewards, routine, and schedule items
- Multilingual support for English, Russian, and Hebrew

The north star is simple: help the child do one small good thing, feel seen, and
come back without shame.
