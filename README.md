# RealoKids

RealoKids is a calm bridge between children, parents, family routines, and the
real world.

Buddy, a gentle bear companion, helps children complete small real-world
missions: move a little, tidy a corner, drink water, take a breath, or connect
with someone nearby. The child does the action in real life, earns stars, and
can redeem them for rewards chosen by the parent.

Parents can use RealoKids before screen time, snacks, stories, or play. One or
two small missions become a bridge into the next thing, without turning the app
itself into the main attraction.

The app can grow with the child. Buddy's tone, mission wording, celebration
style, and guidance can adapt by age, while parents can override settings when
their child needs something gentler, calmer, or more independent.

The Parent Zone lets parents shape the day in one quiet place: missions,
rewards, morning routines, daily schedule blocks, breathing tools, Buddy
behavior, and small notes about what motivates or comforts the child. It can be
protected with a PIN.

RealoKids is designed to feel warm and playful, but not overstimulating. It uses
small wins, simple choices, optional voice guidance, and quiet celebration
moments to help children feel capable.

Current highlights:

- Daily missions for movement, care, tidying, calm, and connection
- Parent-managed rewards and optional before-reward mission bridges
- Morning routine and day schedule support
- Breathing together with Buddy
- Optional text-to-speech guidance
- Age-aware mission wording, Buddy tone, and tiny facts
- Parent-authored child preferences for motivation and comfort
- Custom pictures for missions, rewards, routine, and schedule items
- Multilingual support for English, Russian, and Hebrew

The north star is simple: help the child do one small good thing, feel seen, and
come back to real life without shame.
